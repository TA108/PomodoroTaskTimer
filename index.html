What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method designed to improve focus and productivity. It works by breaking work into short intervals called Pomodoros, typically 25 minutes long, followed by short breaks.
After completing four Pomodoros, you take a longer break. This technique helps you maintain focus, avoid burnout, and increase overall productivity.

How to Use the Pomodoro Timer

  1. Start Your Timer:
    • Click the Start button to begin a 25-minute Pomodoro session.
    • Focus solely on your task during this time.
  2. Short Break After 25 Minutes:
    • When the timer reaches 0:00, the Pomodoro session ends.
    • A short 5-minute break will begin automatically.
    • Use this time to rest, stretch, or grab a drink.
  3. Repeat Pomodoro and Break Cycle:
    • After your short break, click Start again to begin a new Pomodoro session.
    • Continue this cycle: 25 minutes work, 5 minutes break.
